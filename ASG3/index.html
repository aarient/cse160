Ueno Park at Sunset

CSE 160 - Assignment 3: First-Person Voxel World

Note to grader: This scene is directly inspired by Ueno Park in Tokyo, Japan. The cherry blossom path, the red tori gate, and the pagoda style temple are all drawn from that park at golden hour and when the cherry blossoms are in season. It is a 48x48 blocky version. A tall slim red tori gate with sits in the middle of the path, with a red-and-black temple at the far end. Cherry blossom trees line both sides of the path, and a little village of shops with wood, cream, teal, and charcoal walls sits behind the trees. Babe the Blue Ox (carried over from Assignment 2) is grazing in front of the gate, joined by an orange fox + small fox kit, a Nara-style deer, and a chubby panda. All animals walk in their own loops and the pet counter floats in the top-left of the world.

Story / game: Walk over to Babe and press space to pet him (he will sit for ten seconds and longer if you keep petting). Walk up to a deer and press space to feed it. Both counters are shown in the floating panel inside the world.

What is in the world

48x48 voxel park with dirt perimeter walls, stone corner towers, a tall slim red tori gate, a tiered red-and-black temple, two rows of cherry blossom trees, and a small village of shops with four different modern wall textures. Twelve textures total (grass, dirt, stone, bark, blossom, red, black, gold, wood, cream, teal, charcoal). The sky is a solid sunset color.
