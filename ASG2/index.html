Babe the Blue Ox

CSE 160 - Assignment 2: Blocky 3D Animal

Note to grader: This is Babe the Blue Ox from Paul Bunyan. I was inspired to make a blue Ox since I was assigned an Ox for this assignment and I have gone on a roadtrip to Klamath, CA where there is a huge statue of a Blue Ox and Paul Bunyan. In the story of Paul Bunyan, a large Blue Ox is his companion.

Functionality wise: The third-level joint is the front-left leg. Drag to rotate, Shift+click for the poke. Cylinders are used for the horns.
